Fish

Ingredients

  • Fish fillet (tilapia, cod, or your choice)
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Turmeric (optional)
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

1. Marinate the fish with lemon juice, garlic, salt, pepper, and turmeric for 15–30 minutes.
2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
3. Cook the fish for 3–4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through.
4. Serve hot with rice, salad, or vegetables.
