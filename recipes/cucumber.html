Cucumber

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Salt
  • Lemon Juice (optional)
  • Chaat Masala (optional)

Instructions

1. Wash and peel the cucumber if desired.
2. Slice or chop into pieces.
3. Sprinkle with salt and a little lemon juice for added flavor.
4. Optionally sprinkle some chaat masala for a tangy twist.
5. Serve fresh and chilled.
