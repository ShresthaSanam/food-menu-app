Lassi

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Water or Milk
  • Sugar or Honey (optional)
  • Cardamom powder (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

1. In a blender, combine yogurt, water (or milk), and sweetener if using.
2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
3. Add a pinch of cardamom powder for flavor if desired.
4. Serve chilled over ice cubes.
