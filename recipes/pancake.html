Pan Cake

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Maple Syrup

Instructions

1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, eggs, and milk.
2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
3. Pour a scoop of batter and cook until bubbles form.
4. Flip and cook the other side until golden.
5. Serve with maple syrup.
