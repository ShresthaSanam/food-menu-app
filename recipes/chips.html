Chips

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Optional: Paprika, black pepper, garlic powder

Instructions

1. Thinly slice potatoes and soak in water for 30 minutes.
2. Drain and pat dry thoroughly.
3. Heat oil in a pan and fry in batches until golden and crisp.
4. Remove and sprinkle with salt and spices.
5. Let cool slightly and enjoy!
