Malpuwa

Ingredients

  • All-purpose Flour
  • Semolina (optional)
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Oil or Ghee (for frying)

Instructions

1. Mix flour, semolina, sugar, milk, fennel seeds, and cardamom powder into a smooth batter.
2. Let the batter rest for 15â€“30 minutes.
3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan.
4. Pour small ladlefuls of batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown on both sides.
5. Drain excess oil and serve warm.
