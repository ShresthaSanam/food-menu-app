Dal Bhat

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Lentils
  • Turmeric
  • Salt

Instructions

1. Cook rice with water until soft and fluffy.
2. Boil lentils with water, turmeric, and salt until soft.
3. Optionally, temper lentils with garlic and cumin for extra flavor.
4. Serve hot lentil soup (dal) with rice (bhat).
