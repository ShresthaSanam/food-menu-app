Wai Wai Chauchau

Ingredients

  • Wai Wai noodles
  • Oil
  • Chopped Onion
  • Chopped Tomato
  • Chili (optional)
  • Wai Wai masala

Instructions

1. Crush the Wai Wai noodles slightly while still in the pack.
2. Heat a small amount of oil in a pan.
3. Add chopped onion, tomato, and chili. Sauté until soft.
4. Add the Wai Wai noodles and mix well.
5. Sprinkle the Wai Wai masala on top and stir-fry for 1–2 minutes.
6. Serve hot as a quick snack or lunch!
