Chatpate

Ingredients

  • Beaten Rice (Chiura)
  • Instant Noodles (crushed, uncooked)
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Tomato (chopped)
  • Cucumber (chopped)
  • Green Chili (chopped)
  • Fresh Coriander
  • Mustard Oil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Chili Powder
  • Chat Masala (optional)

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix chiura and crushed noodles.
2. Add all chopped vegetables and mix well.
3. Drizzle mustard oil and lemon juice on top.
4. Add salt, chili powder, and chat masala (optional) to taste.
5. Mix everything thoroughly and serve immediately.
