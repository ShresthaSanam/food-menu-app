Muffin

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Baking Powder

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
3. Beat in eggs and mix until smooth.
4. Pour batter into muffin molds.
5. Bake for 18–20 minutes or until golden and cooked through.
6. Let cool and enjoy!
