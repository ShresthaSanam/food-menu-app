Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Condensed milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Optional: Chocolate chips, fruit, or nuts

Instructions

1. Whip heavy cream until soft peaks form.
2. Gently fold in condensed milk and vanilla extract.
3. Mix in optional add-ins like chocolate chips or fruit.
4. Pour into a container and freeze for 6+ hours or until firm.
5. Scoop and enjoy!
