Pho

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Beef or chicken (thinly sliced)
  • Beef or chicken broth
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Star anise
  • Cloves
  • Fish sauce
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Fresh herbs (basil, cilantro)
  • Green onion
  • Bean sprouts
  • Lime wedges
  • Chili or hot sauce (optional)

Instructions

1. In a pot, simmer broth with onion, ginger, star anise, cloves, salt, sugar, and fish sauce for 30â€“40 minutes.
2. Soak rice noodles in hot water until soft, then drain.
3. Strain the broth and bring to a boil.
4. Place noodles in a bowl, top with raw sliced meat, and pour hot broth over to cook the meat.
5. Garnish with herbs, green onion, bean sprouts, and lime.
6. Add chili or hot sauce to taste. Enjoy your pho!
