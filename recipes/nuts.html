Nuts

Ingredients

  • Mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews, etc.)
  • Optional: Sea salt
  • Optional: Honey or maple syrup for sweet version

Instructions

1. Enjoy raw mixed nuts as a healthy snack.
2. For roasted nuts, spread them on a baking sheet.
3. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 8–10 minutes, stirring halfway.
4. Optionally, toss with sea salt or drizzle honey/maple syrup after roasting.
