Chowmein

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Soy Sauce

Instructions

1. Boil noodles until just cooked, then drain and rinse with cold water.
2. Heat oil in a pan, add shredded cabbage and carrots, and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
3. Add noodles and soy sauce, then toss everything together until well combined.
4. Cook for another 2–3 minutes and serve hot.
