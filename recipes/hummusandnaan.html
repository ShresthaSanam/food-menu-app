Hummus and Naan

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (cooked or canned)
  • Tahini
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Flatbread or naan
  • Optional toppings: paprika, parsley, zaâ€™atar

Instructions

1. In a blender, combine chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, and salt.
2. Blend until smooth. Add water to adjust texture if needed.
3. Serve in a bowl, drizzle olive oil and sprinkle optional toppings.
4. Warm naan or flatbread and serve alongside the hummus.
