Popcorn

Ingredients

  • Popcorn kernels
  • Oil (vegetable or coconut)
  • Salt
  • Optional: Butter, seasoning (cheese powder, caramel, etc.)

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add popcorn kernels and cover with a lid.
3. Shake the pot occasionally as the kernels pop.
4. When popping slows, remove from heat.
5. Season with salt and optional butter or flavorings.
