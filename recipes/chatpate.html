Choila

Ingredients

  • Buff or Chicken (boiled or grilled)
  • Mustard Oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Chili Powder
  • Turmeric
  • Fenugreek Seeds
  • Salt
  • Lemon Juice

Instructions

1. Cut the cooked meat into bite-sized pieces.
2. In hot mustard oil, fry fenugreek seeds until dark and remove seeds.
3. Add minced garlic, ginger, chili powder, turmeric, and salt to the hot oil.
4. Mix the oil spice mixture with meat, add lemon juice, and combine well.
5. Serve warm or at room temperature with beaten rice (chuira).
