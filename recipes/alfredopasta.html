Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. In a pan, heat the heavy cream until it starts to simmer.
3. Add grated parmesan cheese and stir until the sauce thickens.
4. Add the cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly.
5. Serve hot with extra cheese on top if desired.
