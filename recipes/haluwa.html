Haluwa

Ingredients

  • Semolina (Sooji)
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • Milk or Water
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Chopped Nuts (optional)

Instructions

1. Heat ghee in a pan and roast semolina until golden and aromatic.
2. In another pot, warm milk (or water) and dissolve sugar in it.
3. Carefully add the sweetened liquid to the roasted semolina.
4. Stir constantly to avoid lumps.
5. Cook until thick and ghee starts separating.
6. Add cardamom powder and nuts, mix well.
7. Serve warm.
