Sushi

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Fillings (e.g., cucumber, avocado, cooked shrimp, crab, raw fish)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi (optional)
  • Pickled ginger

Instructions

1. Cook sushi rice and season with a mix of rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Let it cool.
2. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat, spread rice evenly, leaving some space at the top edge.
3. Add your choice of fillings in a line across the center.
4. Roll tightly using the mat, then seal with a bit of water.
5. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces with a wet knife.
6. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
