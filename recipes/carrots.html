Carrots

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Salt
  • Lemon Juice (optional)
  • Black Pepper (optional)

Instructions

1. Wash and peel the carrots.
2. Cut into sticks, slices, or shred them.
3. Sprinkle lightly with salt and lemon juice if desired.
4. Optionally add a pinch of black pepper for extra flavor.
5. Serve fresh or pack for a snack.
