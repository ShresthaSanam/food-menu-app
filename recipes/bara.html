Bara

Ingredients

  • Black Lentils
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Oil

Instructions

1. Soak black lentils overnight and grind into a thick paste.
2. Mix in finely chopped garlic, ginger, and salt.
3. Heat oil in a pan and drop a spoonful of batter, flattening it into a small disc.
4. Fry until both sides are golden and crispy.
5. Serve hot with pickles or yogurt.
