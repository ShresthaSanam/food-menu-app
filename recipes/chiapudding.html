Chia Pudding

Ingredients

  • Chia Seeds
  • Milk
  • Maple Syrup

Instructions

1. In a bowl or jar, mix chia seeds and milk.
2. Stir in maple syrup.
3. Let it sit for 5 minutes, then stir again to avoid clumping.
4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
5. Serve chilled with your favorite toppings.
