Chuira

Ingredients

  • Flattened Rice (Chuira/Poha)
  • Yogurt
  • Sugar or Honey
  • Banana (optional)
  • Milk (optional)

Instructions

1. Lightly wash and drain the chuira if it's too dry.
2. In a bowl, mix chuira with yogurt.
3. Add sugar or honey to sweeten as desired.
4. Top with sliced bananas or a splash of milk if preferred.
5. Serve fresh and chilled.
