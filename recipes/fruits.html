Fruits

Ingredients

  • Assorted fresh fruits (e.g., apples, bananas, grapes, berries, mangoes)
  • Optional: Mint leaves, lemon juice, yogurt, honey

Instructions

1. Wash and cut fruits into bite-sized pieces.
2. Combine fruits in a large bowl.
3. (Optional) Add a splash of lemon juice to prevent browning.
4. (Optional) Top with mint leaves, a drizzle of honey, or a dollop of yogurt.
5. Serve fresh and chilled.
