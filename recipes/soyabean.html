Soyabean

Ingredients

  • Soyabean Nuggets
  • Spices
  • Onion

Instructions

1. Boil soyabean nuggets for 5–7 minutes, then drain and squeeze out excess water.
2. In a pan, sauté chopped onions until translucent.
3. Add spices and cook until fragrant.
4. Add the soyabean nuggets and stir-fry for 5–10 minutes.
5. Serve hot with rice or roti.
