Thai Tea Boba

Ingredients

  • Thai tea leaves or Thai tea mix
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk or half-and-half
  • Tapioca pearls (boba)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

1. Brew Thai tea by boiling water and steeping the tea leaves or mix for several minutes.
2. Strain the tea and stir in sugar while hot; let it cool.
3. Cook tapioca pearls according to package instructions and set aside.
4. In a glass, add cooked boba pearls, ice cubes, and pour over the cooled Thai tea.
5. Top with a splash of sweetened condensed milk and evaporated milk.
6. Stir and enjoy!
