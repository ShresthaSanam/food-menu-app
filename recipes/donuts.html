Donuts

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, and warm milk to form a dough.
2. Knead the dough until smooth and let it rise for 1 hour.
3. Roll out the dough and cut into donut shapes.
4. Heat oil in a pan and fry the donuts until golden brown.
5. Let them cool and dust with sugar or glaze if desired.
