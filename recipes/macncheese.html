Mac n Cheese

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Milk

Instructions

1. Cook macaroni according to package directions and drain.
2. In a saucepan, heat milk and stir in shredded cheese until melted and smooth.
3. Add the cooked macaroni and mix until fully coated.
4. Serve hot. Optional: Bake in oven for 10 minutes for a crispy top.
