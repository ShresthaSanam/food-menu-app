Gwar Mari

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Oil

Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, and salt with lukewarm water to form a soft dough.
2. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
3. Heat oil in a pan.
4. Tear small portions of the dough and fry until golden brown.
5. Serve hot with tea or achar.
