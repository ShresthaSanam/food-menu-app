Airfried Chickpeas

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (cooked or canned, drained and dried)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Optional: chili powder, cumin

Instructions

1. Preheat air fryer to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a bowl, toss chickpeas with olive oil and spices.
3. Spread chickpeas in a single layer in the air fryer basket.
4. Cook for 12–15 minutes, shaking halfway through, until crispy.
5. Let cool slightly and enjoy as a snack or topping.
