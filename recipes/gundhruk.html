Ghundruk

Ingredients

  • Ghundruk (fermented leafy greens)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Green Chili
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Water

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onion, garlic, and green chili.
2. Add turmeric, chopped tomatoes, and cook until soft.
3. Add ghundruk and mix well.
4. Pour a little water and let it simmer for 5–7 minutes.
5. Adjust salt and serve warm with rice or roti.
