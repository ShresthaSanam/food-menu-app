Samyang Noodles

Ingredients

  • Samyang Pack
  • Egg (optional)

Instructions

1. Boil water in a pot and cook the Samyang noodles according to package instructions.
2. Drain the noodles, leaving a little water in the pot.
3. Add the Samyang sauce and mix thoroughly.
4. Optionally, fry or boil an egg and top the noodles with it.
5. Serve immediately for a spicy treat!
