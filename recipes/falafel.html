Falafel

Ingredients

  • Dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Fresh parsley
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Flour (optional)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

1. Drain soaked chickpeas and blend with onion, garlic, herbs, and spices until coarse.
2. Add baking soda and optional flour if mixture is too wet.
3. Form into small balls or patties.
4. Fry in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
5. Serve with pita, hummus, or salad.
