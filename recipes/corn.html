Corn

Ingredients

  • Sweet corn (canned, fresh, or frozen)
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chili powder (optional)
  • Lime juice (optional)

Instructions

1. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add sweet corn and sauté for 5–7 minutes until lightly browned.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and optional chili powder.
4. Drizzle with lime juice and serve hot.
