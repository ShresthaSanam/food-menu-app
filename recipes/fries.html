Fries

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt

Instructions

1. Peel and cut potatoes into sticks.
2. Soak in water for 30 minutes, then pat dry.
3. Heat oil in a pan and fry potatoes until golden and crispy.
4. Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with salt.
5. Serve hot with ketchup or your favorite dip!
