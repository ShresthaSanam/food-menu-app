Sparkling Water / Soda

Ingredients

  • Chilled sparkling water (plain or flavored)
  • Optional: Lemon or lime slices
  • Optional: Fresh mint leaves
  • Optional: A splash of fruit juice or natural sweetener

Instructions

1. Pour chilled sparkling water into a glass.
2. Add lemon or lime slices for extra flavor if desired.
3. Optionally, add a few mint leaves or a splash of fruit juice.
4. Stir gently and enjoy immediately while bubbly!
