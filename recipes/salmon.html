Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Fresh herbs (like parsley or dill)

Instructions

1. Pat salmon dry and season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté briefly.
3. Add salmon, skin-side down, and cook 3–4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through.
4. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve warm.
