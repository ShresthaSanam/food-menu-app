Wrap / Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Tortilla or Bread
  • Protein Filling (e.g., Chicken)
  • Veggies

Instructions

1. Lay the tortilla flat or use sandwich bread slices.
2. Add your cooked protein filling (like grilled chicken or tofu).
3. Top with your choice of veggies (lettuce, tomato, cucumber, etc).
4. Optionally add sauces or cheese.
5. Fold the wrap or stack the sandwich and serve!
