Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Veggies/Meat

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet or stone.
3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
4. Add shredded cheese and your choice of veggies or meat.
5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
6. Slice and enjoy!
