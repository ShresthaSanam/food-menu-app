Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • Optional: Honey or maple syrup for sweetness
  • Optional: Ice cubes for a colder smoothie

Instructions

1. Add banana, peanut butter, and milk to a blender.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Add honey or maple syrup if desired for extra sweetness.
4. Add ice cubes and blend again for a chilled smoothie.
5. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
