🍚 Jaulo

🟢 Easy | 💆 Comforting | ⏱️ 30 mins

  • 1/3 cup rice
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 cup daal (any)
  • Salt
  • Cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • Ginger (chopped)
  • Veggies of choice
  1. Wash lentils and rice thoroughly until water runs clear.
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker on medium heat.
  3. Add cumin seeds and sauté briefly.
  4. Add garlic and ginger; cook until fragrant.
  5. Stir in lentils and rice. Add turmeric and garam masala.
  6. Add 5 cups of water and salt to taste.
  7. Add chopped veggies of your choice.
  8. Pressure cook for about 15 minutes or simmer until everything is soft and creamy.
  9. Stir occasionally and adjust water as needed. Garnish with cilantro.

🍝 Pasta

🟡 Quick | 👨‍🍳 Medium | ⏱️ 25 mins

  • Pasta (half a bag)
  • 1/2 bottle marinara sauce
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup peas and carrots
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt, cumin, coriander, chili powder
  • Veggies (optional: broccoli, mushroom)
  • 3 eggs
  1. Boil 8 cups of water. Add salt, oil, and pasta.
  2. Cook pasta for 14 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  3. Fry eggs in a pan and keep aside.
  4. Sauté garlic and onions in oil. Add peas, carrots, and optional veggies.
  5. Add marinara sauce and bring to a boil.
  6. Add cooked pasta and mix well.
  7. Add fried eggs and stir everything together. Serve warm.

🥣 Oatmeal

🟢 Easy | 💪 Healthy | ⏱️ 10 mins

  • 1 cup oats
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/4 cup walnuts
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  1. Combine oats, milk, and salt in a pot. Bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and simmer 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in blueberries, walnuts, vanilla, and maple syrup.
  4. Cook until creamy. Serve warm.

🍵 Zeera Pani

🟢 Easy | 🌿 Detox | ⏱️ 15 mins

  • 1 tsp whole cumin seeds
  • 3 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Pinch of turmeric
  1. In a saucepan, heat the water over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds, garlic, turmeric, and salt to taste.
  3. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat and steep for 4–5 minutes.
  5. Strain and serve warm.

🍲 Soup

🟠 Nourishing | 👩‍🍳 Moderate | ⏱️ 30 mins

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 2 ribs celery, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary
  • 5 cups chicken stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 lb boneless chicken thighs
  • 3/4 cup pasta
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onion, carrots, and celery until soft.
  3. Add garlic, thyme, rosemary; stir for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Add stock, bay leaves, and chicken. Bring to a boil.
  5. Simmer 10–12 mins until chicken is cooked. Remove and shred.
  6. Add pasta and cook until tender.
  7. Return chicken, stir in lemon juice. Season and serve hot.
